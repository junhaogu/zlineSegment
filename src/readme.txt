image_background_clean utilize image batch processor in MATLAB to clean image background and output clean image.

repeat_fill_image runs in MATLAB, it flips and shift single cell images and their masks and repeat-fill to a uniform image of 1024*512 pixels image

rescale runs in imagej macro, it crops the output of ZlineDetection algorithm(has background margin) and rescale it to 512*256 pixels image

overlay_fibroblast runs in imagej macro, overlay fibrobalst image to cardiomycoyte images with random intensity above 40
